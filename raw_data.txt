
ARCHETYPE COLORS & CATEGORIES
---

1. Infrastructure Integrators (#1F8C7D - Teal)
   - Connect supply and demand at scale
   - Provide backbone systems
   - Examples: Airlines, hotels, OTAs, GDS platforms

2. Vertical Specialists (#E85D75 - Pink/Red)
   - End-to-end solutions within single segment
   - Specialized expertise
   - Examples: Hotel PMS systems, revenue management tools

3. Experience Designers (#F5A623 - Orange)
   - Create memorable activities
   - Engage travelers emotionally
   - Examples: Tour operators, restaurants, attractions

4. Facilitators & Enablers (#4A90E2 - Blue)
   - Remove friction in transactions
   - Enable other players
   - Examples: Payment processors, cloud infrastructure, APIs

5. Community Builders (#BD10E0 - Purple)
   - Create spaces for interaction
   - Build knowledge and loyalty
   - Examples: Social media, travel communities, review platforms

6. Regulators & Standards Setters (#7ED321 - Lime Green)
   - Define rules and enforce compliance
   - Certify quality
   - Examples: UNWTO, government tourism boards, industry associations

7. Market Aggregators (#B8E986 - Light Green)
   - Wholesale buyers and resellers
   - Link suppliers to intermediaries
   - Examples: Bed banks, wholesale platforms, tour operator networks
